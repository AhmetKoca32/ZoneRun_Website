ZoneRun Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2025

1. Data We Collect

The ZoneRun app may collect or process the following types of data:

  • Location data: Location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) is used for run and area tracking, and for drawing routes and polygons on the map. This data is used for map display and statistics.
  • Account information: Firebase Authentication is used for optional sign-in. Email address and name (if provided by the user) are processed for account management.
  • Device and app information: If Firebase Analytics is used, this may include device type and app usage information.
  • Notification preferences: Preferences and timing for scheduled notifications are stored only on your device and are not sent to our servers.

2. Purpose of Use

Collected data is used for:

  • Calculating and displaying run and area statistics on the map
  • Account creation, sign-in, and account management
  • Improving the app experience (when analytics is used)

3. Storage and Sharing

Your data may be processed and stored on servers using Firebase (Google) infrastructure. Your data is not sold to third parties. We do not share your data with third parties except where required by law (e.g. court order or lawful request by authorities).

4. Data Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Firebase applies its own security measures. We aim to ensure that sensitive information is transmitted and stored securely.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your account from within the app. For requests regarding access, correction, or deletion of your data, please use the contact address below.

6. Children

ZoneRun is not directed at any specific age group. Suitability of the app for younger users is the responsibility of parents or guardians.

7. Changes

When this privacy policy is updated, this page and the "last updated" date will be revised. Significant changes may be announced in the app or by email.

8. Contact

For privacy and data requests: zoneruun@gmail.com
